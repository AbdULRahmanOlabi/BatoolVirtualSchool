F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Here You Can Find The Answer To All of Yours Questions.

  • What are The Tuition Fees For All Levels in Batool Virtual School?

    Preparatory Class -> $500, The First Grades To The Eighth Grades -> $700, The Ninth Grades To The Eleventh Grades -> $1000, Twelfth Grades -> $1200

  • What are The Papers Required To Register in Batool Virtual School?

    Student Photo, The Last Scientific Certificate Obtained By The Student, One of The Following Options:

    1. (A copy of the father's ID + a copy of the mother's ID + extract of civil status)
    2. (A copy of the student's passport + a copy of the father's ID + a copy of the mother's ID)
    3. (A copy of the mother`s page in the family book + a copy of the father`s page in the family book + a copy of the student`s page in the family book)

  • What is The Regestration Mechanism in Batool Virtual School?

    The student Fills Out an Application For Affiliation With The School According To The Enrollment and Admission Instructions, and After Approving and Attaching The Papers Required For Registration, He Opens a File For The Student and an Electronic Account, Then The Informatics Department in The School Sends The Username and Password of Each Student To Enter The Educational Platform.
